Interesting facts about Camels

  • Camels have furry ears
  • Camels can easily move on the desert because of their specially designed feet
  • When they find water they will drink as much as possible

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are one of the only animals to pass the mirror test which is a test of self recognition
  • Pigeons have excellent navigational skills
  • Pigeons are very sociable animals as they are often seen in flocks

Interesting facts about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story of Teddy Roosevelt
  • The first Teddy bear was made by Margarete Steiff
  • The term Teddy bear was first used in the year 1906

Interesting facts about Pandas

  • Pandas are black and white animals that are found in thick bamboo forests
  • Pandas are omnivores animals
  • Pandas do not hibernate

Interesting facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas are also known as tiny violins
  • Cicadas make noise like toy frogs
  • Cicadas are very small in size
